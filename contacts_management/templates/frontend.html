Contacts Manager

Chat Commands: • Say 'Chat [friend name]' to start a chat • Say 'Accept' to accept incoming chat requests • Say 'Reject' to reject incoming chat requests • Say 'End chat' to end current chat • Say 'Friends' to see your full friend list • Say 'Menu' to show menu Note: You can only connect with someone if both of you have stored each other in their contacts.
